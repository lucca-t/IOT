The Garden

Since 2011

The Garden is a constantly evolving band originally from Orange County, California. Established in 2011 by Wyatt and Fletcher Shears. They created the term, "Vada Vada" in 2011 to represent their music and other creations. Vada Vada is a term that represents total freedom of expression without boundaries or guidelines of any sort. They have become known for their energetic and off the wall performances around the world. (El link es la foto).
They're the best because of how free they are with their music and the way that they experiment so much with their sound. I love their 80s OC Punk influences from Minutemen and the way that they take risks with their sound and always push the boundaries. Also the number and variety of spinoffs within VadaVada from them and from their friends.
